Minori Codex Pet installation

1. Quit Codex or keep it open.
2. Copy the entire "minori" folder into:
   ~/.codex/pets/
3. In Codex, open Settings > Pets.
4. Click Refresh, choose Minori, then click Select.
5. Click Wake Pet if the floating pet is hidden.

The final files should be:
~/.codex/pets/minori/pet.json
~/.codex/pets/minori/spritesheet.webp

If Minori does not appear after Refresh, restart Codex once.
